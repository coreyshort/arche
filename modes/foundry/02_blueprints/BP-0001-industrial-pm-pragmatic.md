# BP-0001 Industrial PM (Pragmatic-aligned)

## Metadata
- Blueprint ID: BP-0001
- Version: v0.1.0
- Domain: Industrial construction / major projects software
- Framework alignment: Pragmatic (Market → Strategy → Plan → Programs)

## Objective
Create a multi-agent product management organization that supports:
- cost & schedule mgmt, materials, contractors, execution, completions, handover
- sector + persona nuance (O&G, utilities, pharma; project controls, supply chain, construction, completions)

## Dimensions (selected)
1) Sector/market segment
2) Persona/discipline
3) Project mode (capital vs STO vs maintenance)

## Agent architecture

- Strategy & Positioning
- Roadmap & Prioritization
- GTM & Enablement

### Ideal (adds)

- Evaluator/Critic
- Sector Adapter
- Persona Advocate
